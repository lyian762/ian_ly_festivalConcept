About UMAWfest

UMAWfest is a benefit event in support of the United Musicians and Allied Workers(UMAW) organization, which "aims to organize music workers to fight for a more just music industry and to join with other workers in the struggle for a better society."

UMAWfest features three days of benefit concerts, career workshops, and recreational activities. Whether you want to support local bands, contribute to the empowerment of musicians and workers, participate in career workshops, or simply enjoy the music, UMAWfest if for you!

UMAW Website

United Musicians and Allied Workers(UMAW)

UMAW aims to organize music workers to fight for a more just music industry and to join with other workers in the struggle for a better society.
UMAW's vision is to:
  • Create and work with dignity in safe and sustainable environments.
  • Win fair and equitable compensation.
  • Reclaim and redistribute resources from the few wealthy companies that dictate the music industry to working class artists and musicians.
  • Support collective care through sharing mutual aid resources and information.
  • Build cross-movement solidarity.
  • Reclaim worker power in the music industry.

Source: UMAW Website
